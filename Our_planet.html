Our planet - our future!

Welcome to Our Planet, Our Future - an interactive exhibition where kids become Earth’s little superheroes! Here, you’ll discover how amazing our planet is and learn fun ways to take care of it.

This exhibition is all about understanding how our choices affect the world—and how even small actions can make a big difference. Kids will see that protecting nature isn’t just important, it’s exciting, creative, and fun!

Together, we can keep our planet healthy, colorful, and full of life. After all, the future of Earth is in your hands!

The Recycle Rocket

Work together to place the waste where it belongs! Using four strings, you need to pick up the waste and place it in the right container. It may seem easy, but it takes a lot of focus and collaboration-skills to move the items in the right direction.

The Storm

Gather around the table and create the perfect storm! Learn how positive and negative loaded cloads affect the weather! When everyone is working together you can create the perfect storm with a lot of wind, thunder and rain!

The Whisper game

Can you hear the voice from the other side? Try to communicate with each other through the satelites placed in different rooms.

Is it electric?

The glass dome creates plasma rays when you touch it, creating a fantastic light show! You can even feel the vibrations from the lightning against your fingers. Do you dare to touch it?
