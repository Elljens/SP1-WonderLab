Welcome to Café Wonder!

Nestled within the heart of the museum, our café offers a warm and inviting space to pause, reflect, and enjoy. Whether you’re here for a quick coffee before exploring the museum or a relaxed lunch after your visit.

We serve child-friendly dishes and seasonal pastries, in addition to a unique coffee bar and a wide selection of other beverages.
