Our planet Robots

Have fun at the playground!

Welcome to the Science Playground—a place where curiosity, play, and discovery come together! Here, kids can jump, build, spin, splash, and experiment while learning how science works in everyday life.

he Science Playground turns learning into an adventure—where play is the experiment and fun is the result. It’s a place where every “What if?” leads to discovery, and every discovery sparks the imagination of future scientists, inventors, and dreamers.

Skipping rope

Let the robots skip the rope for you, and test your endurance.
The robot keeps count on the jumps, and tells you how much energy you use during the skipping.

Ride the motor cycle

Explore the fun in riding the police motor cycle. This ride makes you experience the power of speed and and how it feels when air resistance is increased.

Domino

Come and build the biggest domino you’ve seen. With thousands of bricks and a room full of lanes, bridges and stairs, the only thing that can limit you is your imagination. How far can you go?

Hydropower

Explore how water can be turned into electricity in our popular hydropower activity! Here children get to be little engineers and experiment with real water in motion. A perfect activity for curious children
