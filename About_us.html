About us

At the Wonder Lab, we believe science is best learned through play, curiosity, and discovery. Our museum is a hands-on space where children and families can explore the wonders of science, technology, engineering and art in a way that sparks imagination and inspires lifelong learning.

The interactive exhibits is designed to encourage kids to ask questions, make connections, and see themselves as young scientists, inventors, and problem-solvers.

We are more than just a museum—we are a community space where families, educators, and curious minds of all ages come together to explore how science shapes our world. Our mission is to nurture creativity, encourage exploration, and make science accessible and exciting for everyone.

At Wonder Lab, the future of discovery is in the hands of our youngest explorers—and we’re here to guide the journey.

How to get here

There are loads of great ways to get to Wonder Lab – take the tube, grab a taxi or ride the bus.

Need to drive? No problem - we’ve got 2,500 parking spaces within a 5-minutes walk from the museum.

There are also car parks for Blue Badge holders right in front of our building.
