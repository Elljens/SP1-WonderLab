Step into the future - Virtual Reality

Put on your headset and get ready to explore worlds you’ve only dreamed of! Our Virtual Reality Adventure Zone lets kids dive into oceans, travel through space, walk with dinosaurs, and even build entire universes—all without leaving the museum.

Virtual reality isn’t just fun—it helps us imagine the impossible, learn in new ways, and see the world through a whole new lens. This exhibition shows how technology and imagination work together to make dreams feel real.

VR Gaming

Are you a real gamer? What if you can actually BE the player in one of the games? Put the gear on and explore the fun in gaming on a whole new level!

VR Sports

Do you like football? Tennis? Bowling? Or perhaps boxing? Choose from a huge selections of sports and start playing!

Timezone

Have you ever wondered how it is to fly a spaceship? In our Timezone VR simulator you can take a tour in space, being in control of you very own spaceship.
