Welcome to WonderLab Science Museum!

Step into a world where curiosity comes alive! Our brand-new science museum is designed just for kids (and the young at heart) to discover, play, and explore the wonders of science. From hands-on experiments to interactive adventures, every corner is built to spark imagination and inspire big questions. Whether you’re saving the planet, digging into the playground, or exploring the mysteries of robots and technology, there’s always something new to learn and explore. Come ready to touch, tinker, and wonder—because here, science isn’t just something you read about, it’s something you do!
