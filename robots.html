Meet the robots!

Step into a world where machines come to life! Our Robots Alive! exhibition is a hands-on adventure designed just for kids. Meet robots that can walk, talk, draw, and even play games. From tiny bots that follow your commands to giant machines that look like they’ve come from the future, every robot has a story to tell.

Children can explore how robots are built, discover the science behind their movements, and even try coding simple commands to see their own robot creations in action. The exhibition is full of interactive stations where curiosity is the key—and play is the best teacher.

You are in control

With the advanced digital control board you can control the robot to do what you want! Make the robot talk, wave, or even dance! It is all up to you!

Robotrack

Build a ball track with Robotrack! The building blocks are designed to maximize the fun while teaching basic physics principles such as gravity, magnetism and kinetics.

AIBO

Meet AIBO, our own special robot. With a combination of advanced robotics and AI technology we’ve created a perfect little learning partner. Ask him questions, have a conversation, or play games with him.
